Key Features

AI-Powered Queries

Get trade-specific answers from XAI’s Grok API using text, voice, or images. Type questions like “How to fix a clogged drain?” and choose full or summarized responses. Use a 2-second long tap for hands-free voice queries, perfect for noisy job sites, or upload or take photos to identify components like plumbing parts or ask about issues and how to fix them based on the photos.

Job Dashboard

Manage jobs with details like client name, address, and status. View jobs in a list or on a map with markers, navigate via Google Maps, and undo deletions with a single tap. Organize tasks, notes, and photos for seamless workflow.

Material Calculators

Calculate quantities for drywall, roofing, concrete, and more, with wastage estimates included. Fetch local pricing, save estimates to jobs, and switch between metric and imperial units based on your locale.

Daily Joke

Start your day with a trade-related joke, refreshed daily. A light-hearted touch to keep you engaged while tackling tough jobs.
